Welcome to twogreedyguysgetmiffed.com

On this website you can read the novel Two Greedy Guys get Miffed or learn about the authors Brandon Shien and Jacob Pawlak.

This book was written during the years 2017-2018, by high school students Brandon Shien and Jacob Pawlak. Brandon wrote the chapters in the POV of Frank Shek, denoted by (FS). Jacob wrote the chapters in the POV of John Billingston, denoted by (JB)
